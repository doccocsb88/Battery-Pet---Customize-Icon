Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

This application provides battery icon customization, premium subscription access, and optional accessibility-based overlay features. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app processes, why it is used, and what controls are available to the user.

Information We Process

  • On-device configuration data: selected icon style, sticker choices, template choices, and local premium unlock state.
  • Purchase state from Google Play: the app uses Google Play Billing to determine whether weekly or lifetime premium access is active.
  • Runtime system state: battery percent, charging state, connectivity state, signal level, date, and time may be processed on-device to render the overlay.
  • Accessibility service events: if you enable the accessibility service, the app may monitor accessibility events needed to display and refresh the overlay experience.

What We Do Not Intentionally Collect

  • We do not intentionally collect your text messages, contacts, photos, or precise location for the core features described in this app.
  • We do not use the accessibility service to read personal conversations or to profile user behavior for advertising.

How Data Is Used

  • To render status bar overlays and premium content you choose to activate.
  • To restore purchases and enforce premium entitlements through Google Play Billing.
  • To preserve your selected local configuration on the device.

Accessibility Permission

The accessibility service is optional. If enabled, it is used for overlay presentation and refresh logic. The service should only be enabled if you want to use those features.

Storage

Configuration and overlay state are stored locally on your device through app storage. Google Play handles payment processing and subscription lifecycle according to its own policies.

Purchases

Weekly and lifetime premium products are processed through Google Play. Pricing, taxes, renewals, refunds, and account controls are subject to Google Play terms and policies.

Your Choices

  • You can disable the accessibility service at any time in Android settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data, subject to Android and device behavior.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Google Play.

Children

This app is not directed to children under the applicable minimum age for contractual purchases without parental or guardian involvement.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes should be reflected in an updated effective date inside this page.
